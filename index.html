About Us

EDB Foundation is a youth-led nonprofit organization in Kenya, focused on empowering communities through education,mental health support, and gender equality.

Our Vission

A society where everyone, regardless of background or gender, has access to equal opportunities and a better future

Our Mission

To Empower individuals and communities through mental health awareness, education, gender equality, and youth development initiatives. We work collaboratively to foster hope and sustainable change

What We Do

  • Mental Health OutreachPromoting mental wellness and breakng stigma through school visits, forums, and online campaigns
  • Educational EmpowermentSupporting access to education for vulnerable youth and offering mentorship programs
  • Gender EqualityAdvocating for women's and girls' rights, menstrual health' and safety
  • Youth EmpowermentLeadership training,life skills, and civic engagement opportunities
  • Community EventsHosting activities that brings people together to share, learn, and grow unity
